Dear Singapore,
what does my future look like?

Our planet is facing a climate emergency

Biodiversity is declining at an alarming rate never seen before in human history. There is nothing natural about losing nature. Our future depends on the decisions we make now.

In 2020, we have a chance to get governments and businesses to do better. We’ve heard enough talk. Now for real action: better policies and practices that protect people and nature.

Let’s make decision makers pay attention

How? By writing a letter to decision makers in Singapore. Our political leaders, businesses, institutions and schools.

In our letter, we’ll urge Singapore to improve policies and practices.

You will not be alone. We will get as many people as possible to write this letter, creating the largest voice for nature in Singapore.




Policies that safeguard our future

To secure a sustainable future, Singapore needs to set strong and science-based targets across our national policies, business practices, as well as in our local institutions and schools.
